\textbf{Question:}
Find the direction and normal vectors of the line $2x=-5y$.\\
\solution\\

direction vector$=m$\\
normal vector$=n$\\
\begin{align}
\frac{x}{-5}&=\frac{y}{2}\\
 m&=\myvec{-5\\2}\\
 n&=\myvec{2\\5}
\end{align}
